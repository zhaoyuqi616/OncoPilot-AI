Liquid biopsy assays based on circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) can support cancer detection, treatment monitoring, and minimal residual disease assessment. Tumor-informed MRD approaches typically identify patient-specific somatic variants from tumor tissue and then track those variants in plasma. Sensitive error suppression and ultra-deep sequencing are critical because variant allele fractions may be extremely low. Longitudinal ctDNA trends can provide early evidence of molecular recurrence before radiographic progression becomes apparent.
